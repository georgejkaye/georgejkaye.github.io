2M12 1410
Frome to Swindon

Traction: Class 158
TOC: Great Western Railway
Route: South Western Expressways , Western Mainlines
Duration: 30 mins (Part 1) / 30 mins (Part 2)
Difficulty: Easy (Part 1) / Medium (Part 2)

Hello driver! Welcome aboard unit 158960, which is currently standing at Frome. Today you’ll be driving 2M12, the 1410 service from Frome to Swindon, calling at Westbury, Trowbridge, Melksham, Chippenham, and Swindon. Unfortunately, you’ve been pathed behind a late-running freight train from Whatley Quarry, so you may be chasing yellows until Westbury! It also looks like there’s some disruption on the GWML, so be prepared for delays!
