Traction: Class 180
TOC: First Great Western
Route: Western Mainlines
Duration: 50 mins
Difficulty: Easy

Hello driver! Welcome aboard unit 180103, which is currently standing at London Paddington. You are driving 1W13, the 0650 service from London Paddington to Great Malvern. In this scenario, you’ll be driving as far as Oxford, calling at Slough, Reading and Oxford. Although rush hour is just beginning, there are no problems reported so enjoy the early morning drive!
