Hello!

Hello! I’m George. Currently I work as a backend software developer as part of the Insurance Products Integration Development team at Acturis Ltd.

From 2019 to 2024 I was a PhD student at the University of Birmingham, under the supervision of Dan Ghica and Miriam Backens, as a member of the University of Birmingham Theory Group, I helped run the weekly seminar from March 2022 to March 2024 and also looked after the group website.

Between 2021 and 2024 I also used to work part time at the Huawei Edinburgh Programming Languages Lab.
